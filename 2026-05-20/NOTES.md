On CLAUDE.md and prompting

- CLAUDE.md is a constitutional patch, not a fact-list.
  - A bad prompt costs you every session. A good one compounds.
- There's great value in letting your prompts be reviewed by Claude, specifically when the outcome is not what you expect, by describing your intent and giving it your prompt it can refine it.
- Models don't like negative framing, better to add a hard positive budget.
  - It requires active presence of the thing NOT to do when requested so. This means that that thing is present in the context, anyhow. When framing positively, one can leave out the not wanted thing completely up front.
